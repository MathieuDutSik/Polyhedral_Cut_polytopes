Here is a list of $86$ orbits of facets of the hypermetric cone $HYP_8$. This list was obtained by computing:
\begin{enumerate}
\item the affine basis of the Gosset polytope and of the new $7$ dimensional extreme Delaunay polytope.
\item The corresponding extreme rays
\item the expression of the corresponding hypermetric vectors.
\item taking the closure by the switching classes.
\end{enumerate}
Also, I added hypermetric facets of the cut cone $CUT_8$ (class 22). It is likely that the list is complete but any help in finding a missed one would be appreciated.

The data are organized by switching classes, with their major representent first.
